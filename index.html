My Favorite TV Shows

The Office (U.S.)

The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that depicts the everyday work lives of office employees at the Scranton, Pennsylvania.

Once Upon A Time

Emma Swan faces her unlikely destiny in a small town called Storybrooke in Maine. Whilst Flashbacks reveal the World of Fairytales where a conflict between Snow White and the Evil Queen threatens to destroy there entire world and all their happy endings.

Community

A former lawyer attends a community college when it is discovered he faked his Bachelor's degree. In an attempt to get with a student in his Spanish class, he forms a Spanish study group. To his surprise, more people attend the study group and the group of misfits form an unlikely community.

Parks and Recreation

The absurd antics of an Indiana town's public officials as they pursue sundry projects to make their city a better place.
